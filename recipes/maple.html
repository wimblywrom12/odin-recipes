Scrumptous Maple Fudge?!

This amazing recipe looks so delicious :)

Maple fudge is such an amazing snack, you might make it your whole dinner!.

Ingrediants:

  • white chocolate chips
  • sweetened condensed milk
  • butter
  • maple flavored extract
  • broken walnuts

Instructions:

  1. Grease an 8x8-inch glass baking dish, and line with an 8x12-inch sheet of parchment paper.
  2. Melt white chips, milk, and butter till smooth in a bowl. Stir maple extract until just combined.
  3. Add in walnuts. Set the mixture in the fridge for one hour or more. Cut into squares
