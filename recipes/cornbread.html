This amazing recipe looks so delicious :)

Cornbread is a food well loved in the south. Try these mouthwatering muffins to remind yourself of home.

Ingrediants:

  • packaged cornbread mix
  • milk
  • sour cream
  • eggs
  • honey
  • sugar
  • melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375F. Mix wet and dry ingredients.
  2. Let batter rest for 20 minutes. Sprinkle sugar on top of muffins.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes. Brush butter onto muffins and let cool.
