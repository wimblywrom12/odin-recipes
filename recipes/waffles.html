Yummy Waffles!

This amazing recipe looks so delicious

Waffles are easy to make. You will need quite a few items though.

Most importantly, you will need a waffle maker. Anyways, onwards towards the recipe!

Ingrediants:

  • eggs
  • flour
  • milk
  • oil
  • sugar
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • vanilla
  • nonstick spray

Instructions:

  1. Make the batter: Whisk the eggs, then add the flour, milk, and oil. Whisk in the sugar. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  2. Make the waffles: Pour the batter onto the hot, prepared waffle iron. Cook until golden brown and repeat with the remaining batter.
